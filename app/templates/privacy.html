Privacy

We store your message, a short preview of the response, the model used, retrieval count, latency, and approximate token counts for input/output, plus an approximate location (city/region/country) derived from your IP at request time. We do not store IP addresses or hashed IPs.

If location lookup is unavailable, we store null values and continue processing your request.
